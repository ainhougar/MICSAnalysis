Overall Comments

This bookdown was created to structure the workflow of my MICS data analyses. It was also thought to easily share these analytical steps & results.

The goal of these analyses is to assess feasibility of MAIHDA models with the children under 5 data. The first focus is interested in data relating to Pakistan. Unlike in other countries, data in Pakistan was delivered by geographical areas.

Four geographical areas are distinguished: Balochistan, Sindh, Punjab & Pakshtukistan.
My thought is that there were independent statistical teams who deployed the data collections in such regions.
Some variables are available in some regions while others are not. For now, this data discrepancy across regions does not concern our analyses.

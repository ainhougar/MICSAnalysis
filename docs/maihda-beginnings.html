Chapter 3 MAIHDA Beginnings

These are the variables and variables names that could be good candidates for MAIHDA modelling:

  • ECDI: Early Childhood Development Index
  • melevel: Mothers Education
  • HL4: Child’s sex
  • UF13: Language of the interview / UF14: Native language of the respondent
  • Sociodemographical indexes?: Wealth (windex5.x/windex10.x), Urban (windex5u.x/windex10u.x), Rural(windex5r.x/windex10r.x),
